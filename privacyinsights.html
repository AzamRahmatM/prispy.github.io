Data Handling Practices

  • Companies often collect user data for targeted advertising, which can be invasive.
  • Data brokers trade your personal information without your knowledge.
  • Privacy policies can be lengthy and complex, making it challenging for users to understand how their data is used.
  • Some websites may sell your data to third parties, impacting your online privacy.

Online Security and Privacy

  • Weak passwords and password reuse are major vulnerabilities, leaving accounts susceptible to unauthorized access.
  • Two-factor authentication (2FA) significantly enhances account security by requiring multiple verification steps.
  • Public Wi-Fi networks are often insecure, making them potential hotspots for data interception and privacy breaches
